The Center for Sustainable Landscape (CSL) building was opened in Dec 2012. It is a
two-level office building, with total floor area around 2000 m2. It has one AHU serving 
the whole building, and the terminal air distribution system is VAV with no reheat.
The target room of this project is room 107N. This area is office+corridor. The population
is 2-3 people.
